Crispy Curried Tofu and Carrots

Yield: 3–4 servings Prep + cook: 55 minutes

This sheet‑pan meal features roasted carrots and onions paired with crispy curried tofu nuggets and a warm garam masala–yogurt sauce. Serve over rice bowls or stuff into warm pita for a simple, satisfying vegetarian dinner. [web:48]

Ingredients

Crispy Tofu and Veggies

    >1 (14‑ounce) block extra‑firm tofu, drained >1 to 1¼ pounds carrots, scrubbed but unpeeled >1 medium red onion, cut into 1‑inch wedges >3 tablespoons extra‑virgin olive oil, divided >2 tablespoons potato starch or arrowroot powder >2 teaspoons Madras curry powder >Kosher salt and black pepper

Garlic–Garam Masala Yogurt Sauce

    >2 garlic cloves, minced >1 (1‑inch) piece fresh ginger, grated >8 ounces (about 1 cup) unsweetened creamy yogurt >1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice, plus extra for serving >1 teaspoon garam masala >Kosher salt and black pepper

Assembly

    >1 serrano or jalapeño chile, thinly sliced (remove seeds for less heat) >A handful of fresh cilantro leaves and tender stems, chopped >Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions

    > Prep the oven. Position oven racks in the upper and lower thirds and preheat to 425°F. Line one rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper; leave the other unlined. > Press and prep the tofu. Cut the tofu into four slabs and place on paper towels; top with more towels and a heavy book or pan. Press for up to 10 minutes while you prepare the carrots. After pressing, crumble the tofu into small, bite‑sized nuggets. > Prep and season the carrots. For medium carrots, halve them lengthwise; for thick ones, quarter lengthwise; for thin ones, leave whole. Cut at a sharp angle into 2‑inch pieces. In a large bowl, toss the carrots and onion wedges with 1½ tablespoons olive oil, about ¼ teaspoon salt, and pepper to taste. > Season the tofu. Move the tofu nuggets into the same bowl. Add the remaining 1½ tablespoons olive oil, potato starch, curry powder, about ¾ teaspoon salt, and pepper. Toss gently until everything is well coated (small broken bits will become extra crispy). > Spread and roast. Spread the carrots and onions onto the unlined sheet pan. Spread the tofu onto the parchment‑lined pan in a single layer so the pieces don’t touch. Place tofu on the upper rack and vegetables on the lower rack. Roast for about 15 minutes, then stir and continue roasting until the carrots are tender and spotty brown, onions are caramelized, and tofu is golden and crispy, about an additional 15 minutes. > Make the yogurt sauce. While the tofu and vegetables roast, whisk the garlic, grated ginger, yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, ½ teaspoon salt, and pepper to taste in a bowl. Adjust seasoning with more lemon, salt, or garam masala as needed. If yogurt is thin, you can stir in a tiny drizzle of olive oil; if too sour, a pinch of sugar helps balance it. [web:48] > Serve. Divide cooked rice among bowls. Top with roasted carrots, onions, and tofu. Drizzle generously with yogurt sauce, then garnish with sliced chile and cilantro. Finish each bowl with a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.

Tip: For a different vessel, spoon the tofu and carrots into warm pita with a spoonful of yogurt sauce instead of serving over rice. [web:48]

Based on the Crispy Curried Tofu and Carrots recipe by Nisha Vora, NYT Cooking.
